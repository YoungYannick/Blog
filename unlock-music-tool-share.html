Unlock Music Tool Share

Overview

Introduction

Unlock Music is a powerful browser-based tool designed to decrypt encrypted music files from various Chinese music platforms. It allows you to unlock your purchased music files directly in your browser without any installation.

Open source project: https://git.unlock-music.dev/um/web

  • Browser-Based: No installation required, works directly in your browser
  • Wide Format Support: Supports multiple encrypted music formats from various platforms
  • Privacy-Focused: All processing happens locally, no files are uploaded to any server
  • Open Source: Transparent code that can be audited by anyone
